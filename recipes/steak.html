Steak

What is steak?

Steak is meat!

How to cook steak:

  1. Pre heat cooking surface to medium/high temperature (grill preferred)
  2. Place steak onto cooking surface
  3. While steak is cooking season to preference with salt and pepper
  4. Cook for 7 minutes per side to achieve medium-rare for a one pound steak
  5. Serve with sides
